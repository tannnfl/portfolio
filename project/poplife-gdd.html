Document Overview

Full production Game Design Document for POPLife — a 2D pixel management simulator. Covers the complete design specification for the game's core systems, authored across the full development cycle for a cross-functional team of 7+ (designers, engineers, artists).

Contents

  • 80+ shop shelves — each with category, pricing tiers, and unlock conditions
  • 40+ NPC profiles — personality, preference sets, and interaction decision trees
  • 5 quest lines (30+ main quests, 20+ side achievements) derived from narrative dialogue design
  • Economy loop specification — supply chain, customer demand, pricing strategy
  • Gameplay loop documentation and cross-team implementation notes
  • System expansion roadmap — mini-quest integration per NPC/shelf

Design Process

The GDD emerged from narrative-first design: NPC dialogue and story context drove what quests existed, which in turn shaped what shelf/item categories needed to be designed. This bottom-up approach kept the economy grounded in character motivation rather than abstract mechanics.
