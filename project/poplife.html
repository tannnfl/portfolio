POPLife

Lead Designer · 2D Pixel Management Sim · PC · 2025–Present
Featured Systems Design Narrative Design Tool Dev Website ↗ Steam ↗ Design Doc ↗

Overview

  • 2D pixel art management sim set in an adult product store — place shelves, manage stock, grow a loyal customer base
  • Dual-currency economy: Money (operations) + Fame (consumable for upgrades, blueprints, and promotions)
  • Each shelf has formula-driven attributes: sell price, stock cost, maintenance fee, and appeal
  • Customer AI: 40+ NPCs each with Interest Levels, Embarrassment Points, and a Money Bag — behavior varies per profile
  • Daily loop: morning restocking decisions → real-time store hours → end-of-day settlement panel
  • Bundle system: 2–4 adjacent shelves auto-activate, boosting appeal and reducing stock costs
  • Store Policies: toggleable daily effects with explicit tradeoffs — Extended Hours, Category Sale, Advertisement, and more
  • Quest system: customer/NPC requests unlock blueprints; VIP Story Missions drive narrative through implicit puzzle dialogue

My Role

  • Designed 80+ shop shelves — each with category, pricing, and unlock conditions
  • Created 40+ NPCs with individual personalities, preferences, and interaction logic
  • Authored 5 quest lines (30+ main quests, 20+ side achievements) derived from narrative dialogue design
  • Led cross-team gameplay discussions and wrote the Game Design Document
  • Coordinated design delivery with engineering and art departments
  • Built Unity Editor tools including a custom quest editor
