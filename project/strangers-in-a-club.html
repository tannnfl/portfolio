Strangers in a Club

Solo Developer
Game Prototype Narrative Unity 2D Play on itch.io ↗

Overview

An interactive game set in a nightclub. Players click on characters to observe them and gain insights into their personalities. Inspired by Lower East Side nightlife.

Mechanics

  • Click on patrons to observe them
  • Interpret character personalities through visual cues — e.g., a security guard who appears tough but is soft inside
  • Each player's interpretation creates a unique experience

Future Vision

  • More poetic interaction
  • Original audio, photos, and GIFs
