Strangers in a Club

Solo Developer · Narrative Design · 2024
Game Prototype Narrative Solo Dev Unity Play on itch.io ↗

Overview

A short observational game set in a nightclub. Players click on characters — a soft-hearted security guard, a dejected figure on a chair, an exuberant dancer — and catch glimpses of hidden inner lives that contrast with surface appearances.

My Role

  • Designed all character concepts and narrative beats
  • Implemented the click-to-reveal interaction system in Unity
  • Created all original 2D artwork for characters and environment
  • Designed the audio atmosphere drawing on personal experience with Lower East Side nightlife

Design Notes

The game deliberately withholds context — players project meaning onto strangers rather than receiving a prescribed story. Future iterations will add more poetic interaction layers and original audio and photographic assets that were produced but not included in the initial build.
