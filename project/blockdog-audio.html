Blockdog Audio Design

Solo Audio Designer
Audio Design FMOD Unity

Overview

Sound effects and dynamic background music designed for the game "Blockdog." Audio was crafted to match the game's jelly-like visual aesthetic.

SFX Design

  • Layered bubble and jelly sounds with dog barks for action audio
  • Pick-up sounds are softer than throw sounds to convey effort
  • Death audio: toned-down and melancholic
  • Block drop: subtle state indicator
  • All repeating SFX use randomized pitch automation

Dynamic Music System

A "danger level" parameter in FMOD tracks the highest block position (1–11). Music density increases with added percussion as danger rises. Three music phases. Stems sourced from freesound.org.
