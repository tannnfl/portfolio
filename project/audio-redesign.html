Audio Redesign: Cooking Scene

Solo Audio Designer
Audio Design

Overview

Redesigned the audio for a cooking scene from the Japanese anime In the Corner of the World. "The sounds in the kitchen are one of people's most familiar sounds, like oxygen. They're beautiful and strong, yet hardly noticeable." Layered multiple cooking and food sound layers to create a warmer, cozier atmosphere than the original.

Approach

Enhanced ambient kitchen soundscapes by adding multiple cooking and food sound layers to intensify the cozy, slice-of-life feeling.
