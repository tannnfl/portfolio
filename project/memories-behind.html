Overview

A flat game about Shanghai's 2022 COVID lockdown. "A flat game is a book made of images, while the singular dimension of linear narrative is extended." Uses photography and light interaction to tell the story.

Story Behind

Inspired by returning to Shanghai after the lockdown — walking familiar but changed streets, noticing lingering infrastructure, wondering about the protesters.
