Oh No! Neko Ate My Eyes!

Solo Developer (Design + Programming)
Game Prototype Audio Design Unity 2D FMOD Play on itch.io ↗

Overview

An audio-focused experimental game. Players locate 5 cats in darkness using 3D spatial sound. Whistle to trigger cat responses; interact with the environment through touch and audio feedback. No visual gameplay — audio is the core.

Mechanics

  • Whistle input and keypress toggles
  • 3D spatial sound detection
  • Pure audio gameplay — no visual gameplay elements
