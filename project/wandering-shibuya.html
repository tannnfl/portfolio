Wandering Shibuya

Solo Level Designer · Unreal Engine · 2025
Level Design Unreal Engine Solo Dev

Overview

An open-world level recreation of Shibuya Station and its surrounding district, built in Unreal Engine. The project follows a structured iteration process — research, sketch, blockout, playtest — with documented feedback loops guiding each revision toward a more authentic sense of urban density and pedestrian flow.

My Role

  • Conducted environmental research and produced reference documentation for the Shibuya district
  • Sketched and iterated on layout plans before entering the engine
  • Built and refined the full blockout across three playtest iterations
  • Added encounter design, detail passes, and pedestrian flow logic in each revision
  • Collected and implemented structured playtest feedback at every stage
