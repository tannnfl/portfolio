Wandering Shibuya: An Open World Attempt

Solo Developer · Level Designer · In Progress
Level Design Unreal Engine 3D In Progress

Overview

An open-world level design project recreating Shibuya Station and its surroundings in Unreal Engine. Development proceeds iteratively through research, blockout, playtesting, and refinement.

Development Process

  1. Research — reference gathering
  2. Block out: The Crossing — initial 3D blockout
  3. Sketching: Full map — conceptual map design
  4. Iteration 1 — recreating the crossing
  5. Playtest 1 — feedback collection
  6. Iteration 2 — completing general blockout
  7. Playtest 2 — second round of feedback
  8. Iteration 3 — details and encounters
