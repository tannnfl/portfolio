Her Path of Falling in Love

Solo Developer
Game Prototype Narrative Unity Play on itch.io ↗

Overview

Inspired by the book cover of The First Bad Man. Explores toxic relationships — "drowning in a manipulative and toxic relationship, where the roses are just a disguise of guns." Final scene: a gun emerging from an angry mouth — a metaphor for verbal abuse and gaslighting.

Mechanics

  • Players can only move, no jumping
  • Stepping down platforms prevents going back up
  • One-way progression emphasizes irreversible commitment
